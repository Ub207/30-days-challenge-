# Task 1: The October 2025 AI Turning Point

**Goal:** Understand the "October 2025 AI Turning Point" and the shift to AI-Driven Development (AIDD).

## Study Material
*   **Preface** of the "AI-Native Book" (Pages 6-12).

## Key Concepts
*   **AI Turning Point:** A predicted shift in late 2025 where AI becomes central to development.
*   **Agentic AI:** AI agents capable of autonomous actions.
*   **EvDD (Evaluation-Driven Development):** A new paradigm focusing on evaluating AI outputs.
*   **AI Productivity Boom:** The expected increase in developer productivity.
